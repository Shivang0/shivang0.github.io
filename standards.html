AI Risk Management Framework

NIST AI RMF 1.0 (2023)

The NIST AI Risk Management Framework provides a comprehensive approach to managing AI risks throughout the AI lifecycle. It establishes governance, risk assessment, and mitigation strategies for trustworthy AI systems.

  • GOVERN: AI governance and oversight
  • MAP: AI risk identification and assessment
  • MEASURE: AI performance and risk metrics
  • MANAGE: Risk mitigation and response

Implementation Roadmap

Step-by-step approach to implementing AI security standards in your organization

  1. Assessment & Gap Analysis
    Evaluate current AI security posture against selected standards. Identify gaps in governance, technical controls, and processes.
  2. Standards Selection & Prioritization
    Choose applicable standards based on your industry, risk profile, and regulatory requirements. Prioritize implementation phases.
  3. Governance Framework Establishment
    Implement AI governance structure, policies, and oversight mechanisms as required by NIST AI RMF and ISO standards.
  4. Technical Controls Implementation
    Deploy security controls for AI development, deployment, and operations following OWASP guidelines and industry practices.
  5. Risk Management Integration
    Integrate AI-specific risks into existing risk management frameworks and establish continuous monitoring processes.
  6. Testing & Validation
    Conduct security testing using our prompt collection and validation against compliance requirements.
  7. Continuous Improvement
    Establish metrics, regular assessments, and update processes to maintain compliance and improve security posture.
